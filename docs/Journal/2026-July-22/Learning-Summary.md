# Learning Summary

Today I learned:

- Software should be designed around business processes, not technology.
- Business analysis comes before coding.
- A single sale can contain multiple products.
- Inventory should be event-driven.
- Payments should be tracked per sale instead of only per customer.
- Product prices should come from a Product Master.
- Discounts belong to the sale, not the product.
- Transport charges should be stored separately.
- AI becomes valuable only after reliable business data is collected.
- Good architecture reduces future development effort.
